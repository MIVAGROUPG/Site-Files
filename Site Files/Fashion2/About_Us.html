About Us

Welcome to Lux and Leisure, where eco-friendly fashion meets elegance. Explore our unique fusion of African heritage and contemporary style, crafted for young trendsetters.
We're a fashion brand passionate about celebrating the beauty of African heritage through our stunning Ankara print designs

Our Story

Lux and Leisure was founded by a group of friends passionate about African culture and fashion. We saw a need for affordable, high-quality Ankara print clothing and decided to create it ourselves. With diverse skills in fashion, design, and business, we combined our talents to showcase African heritage in a unique and stylish way.

Our Mission

At Lux and Leisure we are committed to:

  • - Showcasing the beauty and diversity of African culture through our unique Ankara print designs
  • - Empowering individuals to express their cultural identity with pride and confidence
  • - Providing high-quality, stylish clothing that's both affordable and sustainable
  • - Building a community that celebrates African heritage and promotes cultural exchange

Our Values

  • • - Cultural Authenticity:
    We're committed to honoring the rich cultural heritage of Africa, ensuring that our designs are respectful and true to their roots.
  • • - Quality and Sustainability:
    We use high-quality materials and eco-friendly production methods to minimize our environmental impact.
  • • - Inclusivity:
    We believe that fashion should be for everyone, regardless of age, size, or background..
  • • - Community:
    We're dedicated to building a supportive community that celebrates African culture and promotes cultural exchange.

Our Designs

Our Ankara print designs are inspired by the vibrant colors and patterns of African culture. From bold and bright to subtle and sophisticated, our designs cater to a wide range of tastes and styles. Whether you're looking for a statement piece or everyday wear, we've got you covered.

Join the Lux and Leisure community today and experience the beauty of African culture through our stunning Ankara print designs.
